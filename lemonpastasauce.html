Creamy Lemon Pasta Sauce

Tired of boring old pasta?!

Look no further! This simple and quick recipie will have your tastebuds in a chokehold. Your mouth will be watering from the combination of parmesean cheese, garlic, and lemon. Get started making this delicous pasta sauce with the ingredients below.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 50g parmesean, finely grated
  • 3 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Zest of 2 lemons

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil over medium heat in a fry pan
  2. Add the garlic and let it sizzle gently for a minute, don't allow the garlic to brown.
  3. Turn the heat down slightly and add the cream, bring to a very gentle simmer for a minute or so.
  4. Add the parmesan in a few lots, whisking in between so it melts evenly into the cream before adding the next lot.
  5. Add the salt and pepper and let it gently bubble for a minute or two until it thickens slightly.
  6. Add the lemon juice, then mix

As a side note, everyone likes their sauce a little different. Please feel free to mess around with the ingredients and make the sauce to your liking!
